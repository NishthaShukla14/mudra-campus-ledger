Terms of Use

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to MUD₹A. By accessing this platform, you agree to comply with our campus financial guidelines.

1. Closed-Loop Campus Ledger

MUD₹A is a closed-loop institutional ledger system. The balances reflected in your account represent pre-paid academic credits and are strictly for use within the campus ecosystem (e.g., canteens, libraries, administrative fees). They are not fiat currency.

2. Account Security

Users are solely responsible for the security of their login credentials. Do not share your password or biometric data with anyone. The institution will never ask for your password.

3. Prohibited Activities

Any fraudulent transactions, attempts to bypass the security measures, or usage of the API outside the authorized web client will lead to immediate account suspension and academic disciplinary action.

4. Transaction Reversals

Once a peer-to-peer transfer is completed, it cannot be reversed automatically. For disputes, you must raise a support ticket via the Helpdesk within 48 hours of the transaction.
