Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your privacy is our priority. MUD₹A complies with strict institutional data policies to protect your financial footprint.

1. Data Collection

We only collect data necessary for ledger operation: account credentials, transaction logs, support tickets, and optionally biometric descriptors for FaceID.

2. Biometric Data Processing

If you opt-in to FaceID, we do not store your actual photos. Instead, we extract and securely store an encrypted 128-dimensional mathematical vector. This vector cannot be reverse-engineered into a photograph.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Your data remains entirely within the campus intranet. MUD₹A does not share, sell, or rent any student transaction data to external third parties or vendors.

4. Data Retention

Transaction logs are maintained for the duration of your academic enrollment, plus one academic year, after which they are anonymized for institutional analytics.
